Sunflower

The annual plant of the sunflower (Helianthus annuus) boasts a substantial daisy-like flower face. Derived from Greek roots, its scientific name, Helianthus, fuses "helios" (meaning "sun") and "anthros" (meaning "flower").

jasmine

Jasmine flower gives very little fragrance during the day. But as the night begins to fall. Its fragrance starts spreading. It is very popular among the people due to its fragrance.

Mogra

Mogra is a type of plant whose flowers are very fragrant with small white color. Its botanical name is "Jasminum sambac". The flower of Mogra is called the national flower of the Philippines country.
